Security & HIPAA Compliance

Handling PHI is non-negotiable. We operate with strict HIPAA-aligned processes, secure access controls, and clear data boundaries between your clinic and our team.

HIPAA-Aligned Operations

  • Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) available on request.
  • Restricted PHI access based on role and function.
  • No PHI stored in personal devices or email inboxes.

Secure Workflows

  • EMR access via secure VPN or MFA-protected portals.
  • Internal SOPs for password rotation and access reviews.
  • Audit trails on all claim and AR activities.

The Velden Mission

Velden Health acts as the "Sidecar" to your billing department. We provide specialized liquidity recovery for Illinois behavioral health clinics.

Our Objective

Clinically excellent providers should not be penalized by aged ledgers. We provide the specialized intervention required to convert 60-180+ day denials into cash flow, without distracting your team from current billing responsibilities.

We operate as a quiet "background revenue engine" - precise, persistent, and entirely aligned with your ledger's health.

The Operator Model

  • Start with a 20-Claim Proof of Concept.
  • Plug-and-play integration with TherapyNotes & Kareo.
  • Weekly "Ledger Health" reporting.
  • Performance-based fee structure (We only win when you collect).
